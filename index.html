    Compare & Connect K-12

    Goal: Make insights from school connectivity data widely accessible.

    Context: During my first months at EducationSuperHighway, I spent countless hours creating reports on connectivity gaps in public schools. Each report often took weeks to complete due to frequent design tweaks and changes in underlying data. I set out to build a tool that presents data in meaningful visualizations and makes standardized reporting possible.

    Tools: SQL, R(Shiny, ggplot2, Leaflet)

    Impact: The Connectivity Dashboard was adopted by internal teams across the company. It reduced time required to create connectivity reports to less than a week. It also enabled non-technical colleagues to easily gather and share data insights, helping us work more collaboratively. This tool was also featured in the RStudio blog.

    My Contribution:

    • Pitched the initiative to leadership
    • Designed and built the dashboard UI
    • Gathered user feedback
    • Executed the project from start to finish
